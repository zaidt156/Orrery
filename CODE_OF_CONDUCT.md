# Code of Conduct

Orrery is an open, welcoming project. Everyone — regardless of background or experience — should
feel comfortable taking part.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, patient, and considerate.
- Welcome newcomers and assume good intent.
- Give and accept constructive feedback gracefully.
- Keep discussions focused on the work.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or demeaning comments of any kind.
- Sexualized, violent, or hateful language or imagery.
- Personal attacks, trolling, or deliberately derailing discussions.
- Publishing someone's private information without their consent.

## Scope

This applies to all project spaces — issues, pull requests, and discussions — and to anyone
representing the project in public.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it privately to the maintainers via
GitHub (a direct message, or the repository's **Security** tab). Reports are handled
confidentially. Maintainers may edit or remove content and warn or ban anyone who violates these
standards.
